About Us

Learn more about the mission, purpose, and statewide community impact of the Delaware Juneteenth Association.

Delaware Juneteenth Association

The Delaware Juneteenth Association is a statewide community-based organization of compassionate volunteers who are dedicated to helping and serving people.

Our purpose is to advance the historical, cultural, educational, social, self-esteem building, and knowledge of prevention through programs and activities that serve communities' youth and families.

Community Mission

The Delaware Juneteenth Association works to preserve the meaning of Juneteenth while creating opportunities for celebration, education, service, and cultural pride throughout Delaware.
