Male Talent Showcase

We introduced the Male Talent Showcase in 2017. It derived from the Youth Pageant Family Enrichment Program, now known as the "Male Juneteenth Kingship" Program. It was created for young men ages 8-18 to display their talent in the form of spoken word, musical instruments, singing and dancing.

This event has proven to be very popular and very well received. It is now a gem in the making with plans to take it to another level. Plans are to add an educational component, adding preliminary workshops and activities similar to the Youth Pageant. With this in mind, the program will cater to male ideals and perspectives.
