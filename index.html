The Delaware Juneteenth Association is a statewide community-based organization of compassionate volunteers, who are dedicated to helping and serving people. Our purpose is to advance the historical, cultural, educational, social, self-esteem building and knowledge of prevention. This is implemented through the provision of programs and activities to communities' youth and families.
